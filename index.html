THE HOOK & CONFIRMATION
"Hey [Owner’s Name], I know this is out of the blue, but do you still own the property over at [Property Address]?"
Seller Says: "I'm Not Selling / How'd You Get My Number?"
Pivoting Script: "I completely respect that! Honestly, we find that many owners aren't selling until they hear a risk-free, cash valuation that doesn't cost them a dime in commissions. If you don't mind me asking, if you ever did sell down the line, what would be your goal?"
"Perfect. Look, we buy properties cash, as-is, meaning you don't make any repairs or pay realtor fees. If we could put together a fair, hassle-free cash offer, would you at least take a look at it?"
THE CONDITION DEEP-DIVE
"To make sure I give you a real, accurate number... can you walk me through the house layout? How many beds/baths, and what's the general layout like?"
"If you were to pass a formal home inspection tomorrow, what are the 2 or 3 things that absolutely need to be updated or fixed up?"
Seller Says: "The House Is Absolutely Perfect"
Pivoting Script: "That's fantastic! It sounds like you've really taken pristine care of it. Usually, even gorgeous homes have older roofs, water heaters, or original wiring. If you had to estimate the age of the roof or central air units, where are we at?"
TIMELINE & MOTIVATION DISCOVERY
Why are you considering selling?
"If we hit your exact pricing timeline, when is your ideal moving day? Are we looking to close in 30 days, or do you need a bit more time to transition?"
PRICING ANCHOR
"Completely understanding that we cover all the closing costs, repair fees, and commissions... what's the absolute baseline number you need to walk away with to make this deal make sense for you?"
Seller Says: "Give Me Full Zillow Value"
Pivoting Script: "I completely get wanting to hit that number. Zillow assumes the house is completely renovated, sitting on the market with an agent for 6 months, and that you're paying 6% in commissions plus closing fees out of pocket. Since we buy as-is with cash in 30 days, where can we meet in the middle?"

🚨 PRE-FORECLOSURE ACQUISITION SCRIPT

1. Empathy Hook
2. Foreclosure Deep-Dive
3. Debt Structuring
4. Resolution Blueprint
THE EMPATHY HOOK & SENSITIVE APPROACH
"Hello [Owner’s Name]? My name is [VA Name] with (Your Company). I'm reaching out because our system flagged the property at [Property Address] as showing up on public notices recently. We specialize in helping homeowners navigate stressful timeline situations directly with banks. I just wanted to see if you are looking to stay in the home, or if you're exploring options to transition out smoothly?"
Seller Says: "I'm working with an attorney / My bank is handling it"
Pivoting Script: "That is excellent that you have representation! Attorneys are great for pushing back dates, but they don't bring cash to closing to actually discharge the balance. We work directly alongside owners to establish real backup solutions so you don't face an abrupt exit if your current option stalls out."
TIMELINE & FORECLOSURE DEEP-DIVE
"To ensure we construct the correct game plan, have you been assigned an official Auction or Sheriff Sale date yet by the county or courts?"
"Have you attempted to work out a Loan Modification, Forbearance agreement, or Short Sale directly with your current lender?"
DEBT STRUCTURING & LIEN OVERVIEW
"In order to accurately build out a full payout strategy that leaves your credit intact, do you know roughly what the total reinstatement amount is to bring the mortgage completely current right now?"
"And what is the total remaining payoff balance left on that primary mortgage statement?"
"Are there any secondary liabilities tied to the title that we need to handle for you, like a second mortgage, tax liens, HOA dues, or mechanics liens?"
RESOLUTION BLUEPRINT & CLOSING CAPTURE
"If we can step in, handle your back payments entirely, and coordinate a cash option that allows you to walk away cleanly with credit protection and cash in your pocket to relocate, would that be a path you'd want to run with?"

Thank you for your transparency, [Owner’s Name]. Time is our absolute highest priority right now since the bank works on fixed deadlines. I am going to escalate your file instantly to our Senior Acquisition Specialist.

They will pull the specific title details and formulate an emergency cash blueprint or structural adjustment approach to step between you and the lender.

Hang tight—(Your Company) is on your side, and we will guide you through this step-by-step!

⏱️ EXPIRED LISTINGS SCRIPT

1. Out of the Market Hook
2. Motivation & Alignment
3. Price Discovery
4. Options Closing
OPENING HOOK
You: "Hi, is this [Seller Name]?"
You: "My name is {{Agent Name}} with {{Company name}}. I'm calling about the property on {{Address}}. I noticed the listing came off the market and I was curious—did you still want to sell it, or are you taking a break for now?"
IF THEY WANT TO SELL
You: "Got it. Out of curiosity, what do you think stopped it from selling?"
You: "If you could wave a magic wand and get the property sold, what would that ideally look like?"
MOTIVATION QUESTIONS
"Why were you looking to sell in the first place?"
"Has anything changed since the listing expired?"
"If you found the right solution, how soon would you like to sell?"
"What happens if the property doesn't sell in the next 30 to 60 days?"
TRANSITION FRAME
You: "The reason I ask is that my company buys properties directly. Sometimes we pay cash, sometimes we use creative financing depending on the situation. I'd like to see if we can create a solution that works for both of us."
PRICE DISCOVERY
You: "What was the last list price?"
You: "If we could eliminate commissions, repairs, showings, inspections, and uncertainty, what's the lowest price you'd realistically consider?"
LIVE REBUTTAL WORKBENCH
Objection: "I'm Relisting"
You: "I understand. Before you relist, let me ask you this: if another agent could get it sold, wouldn't the last one have done it already? What do you think will be different this time?"
Objection: "I Want Retail Price"
You: "I completely understand. If I were selling, I'd want top dollar too. My question is: what's more important to you right now—getting the highest possible price or getting the property sold with certainty?"
Objection: "How Did You Get My Number?"
You: "I work with homeowners whose properties recently came off the market. Your information is publicly available through real estate data sources."
APPOINTMENT CLOSING STRATEGY
You: "Based on what you've told me, it sounds like there may be a fit. The next step is simple. Let me take a look at the property and put together some options. If one makes sense, great. If not, no pressure. Does tomorrow afternoon or evening work better?"

The Real Secret: The biggest mistake wholesalers make is trying to get a contract on Call #1.

Top performers often get contracts after: 5–15 follow-ups • Multiple texts • Multiple calls • Building rapport over weeks.

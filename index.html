📞 FULL INTAKE CALL SCRIPT

OPENING
Hi, is this [Owner’s Name]?
Hi [Name], this is [VA Name] calling on behalf of (Your Company). We’re local investors actively buying in your area. I reviewed your property and wanted to see if you were interested in selling.
Did I catch you at an okay time?
Could you tell me more about the property — beds, baths, square footage, and property history?
“Great question! I’m looking to understand the condition, updates, and repairs needed so I can get a clearer picture of the property.”
PROPERTY CONDITION
How old is the roof?
When was the kitchen last updated?
Does the HVAC system work and when was it serviced?
Any foundation issues or cracks?
Any cracks in walls or ceilings?
Anything else needing updates or repairs?
Estimated repair cost?
SELLER MOTIVATION
Why are you considering selling?
“I completely understand. Knowing more about your situation helps us understand how we can best help.”
OCCUPANCY
Do you live there now or is it rented/vacant?
If vacant, how long?
Do you have current photos?
TIMELINE + FINANCIAL
How quickly do you need to sell?
Mortgage or free and clear?
Monthly payment?
Current or behind?
PRICE
Have you thought about what price you'd want?
If hesitant: “That’s okay — even a rough range helps us see if this is worth exploring.”
If seller asks: “What would you offer?”

“That’s a great question. My job is to gather details so our acquisitions manager can run accurate numbers.”
CREATIVE FINANCE
If cash doesn’t work, would you be open to:
Options include novation, owner financing, or taking over mortgage payments.

Thank you, [Owner’s Name], for answering my questions! Next, I'll share this with our Acquisition Manager. He/She will team up with one of our trusted realtors to do a thorough market analysis of your property.

Once that’s done, we’ll get back to you with our offer and talk about how we can help.

Do you have any questions for me so far? Awesome!

Thanks again for choosing (Your Company) — we’re excited to work with you!
